Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Renoriser ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect through this website, how we use it, and your rights under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

What we collect

When you submit our quote or contact form, we collect the information you provide — typically your name, email address, phone number, project type, and any details you choose to share. We do not require you to create an account.

Like most websites, we also use Google Analytics / Google Ads tags, which may collect anonymized usage data (such as pages visited and approximate region) via cookies to help us understand traffic and measure advertising.

How we use it

  • To respond to your quote request or inquiry and follow up about your project;
  • To prepare and send you an estimate;
  • To improve our website and services.

We use your information only for the purposes you'd reasonably expect when contacting a renovation company. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

Who we share it with

Form submissions are delivered to us through Web3Forms, a third-party form-delivery service, and reach us by email. We share information only with service providers that help us operate (such as form delivery and analytics), and only as needed to provide our services.

How long we keep it

We retain inquiry and project information only as long as necessary to serve you and to meet legal or business requirements, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or withdraw your consent at any time. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Doing so will not prevent you from using the site, though some analytics features may not function.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Reach us at renoriser@outlook.com or (365) 476-9000.
